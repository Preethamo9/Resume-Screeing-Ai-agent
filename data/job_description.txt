About the Role

We are looking for a passionate and skilled **AI Engineer** with **2+ years of hands-on experience** in building, deploying, and optimizing AI/ML solutions. As an AI Engineer, you will be responsible for designing intelligent systems, developing machine learning models, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver AI-powered products.
This is an exciting opportunity for someone who thrives in a fast-paced environment and wants to be at the forefront of AI innovation.


Key Responsibilities

* Design, build, and deploy AI/ML models for real-world applications (e.g., classification, recommendation, NLP, computer vision).
* Develop, train, test, and optimize deep learning and traditional machine learning models.
* Preprocess and clean large datasets for training and inference using Python and relevant libraries.
* Work with AI frameworks and tools such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, and Hugging Face Transformers.
* Integrate ML models into production systems using APIs or containerized solutions (e.g., Docker, FastAPI, Flask).
* Conduct experiments and A/B testing to evaluate model performance and effectiveness.
* Monitor model drift and performance in production; apply retraining strategies when needed.
* Collaborate with Data Scientists, Software Engineers, Product Managers, and other stakeholders.
* Stay updated with the latest AI/ML research and industry trends.
* Prepare technical documentation, model reports, and maintain reproducible codebases.

Required Skills & Qualifications

* Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, or a related field.
* **2+ years of experience** as an AI Engineer, ML Engineer, or similar role.
* Strong proficiency in Python and experience with libraries like NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, etc.
* Hands-on experience with ML frameworks such as **TensorFlow**, **PyTorch**, **Keras**, **Scikit-learn**.
* Practical knowledge of **NLP**, **Computer Vision**, or **Generative AI** is a strong plus.
* Experience with **LLMs (e.g., GPT, LLaMA, Mistral)** and fine-tuning techniques is desirable.
* Understanding of data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model evaluation techniques.
* Familiarity with deployment tools like **Docker**, **Kubernetes**, or **MLflow**.
* Experience using **cloud platforms** such as AWS, Azure, or GCP for model training and deployment.
* Ability to write clean, efficient, and well-documented code.
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
